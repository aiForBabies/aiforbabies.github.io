Quantization

Quantization

What is it

A technique that makes AI models smaller and faster by reducing the precision of their internal numbers.

The analogy

Original: a 4K movie on Blu-ray — stunning, massive, requires a dedicated player. Quantized: a compressed streaming version — nearly identical to most viewers, runs on anything, a fraction of the size.

At your job

Quantized models run on cheaper hardware or on-device (your laptop, your phone). If your tech team is evaluating local AI deployment — running models on-premise instead of cloud — quantization is part of that conversation.

The one thing to remember

Some quality is lost in quantization, usually imperceptibly. For most tasks, a quantized model is good enough and significantly cheaper to run.
